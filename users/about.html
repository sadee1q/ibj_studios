ABOUT IBJ STUDIO

IBJ Studio Nigeria is a modern photo studio. IBJ Studio is equipped with ultra-modern photography equipment and manned with skilful and experienced photographers. We specialise in professional photo shoots for individuals and businesses, and our photography services cut across many industries.

Whether you are looking for professional wedding photographer, business headshots, event photography, products shoots, family shoot or model portfolio shoots, our photographers are on-ground to deliver the services to you at best.

We do not stop at only offering professional photography services in adamawa and its environment, we also provide videography, virtual tour, training, photo editing, studio hire, meeting room hire, photo booth hire and video booth hire services in Nigeria. Therefore, you can trust that all the images we capture for you will be timeless and perfect.

Moreover, because we are more than a photography company – we are a full-service digital and multimedia company – we help businesses to meet all their digital communication and PR needs. We work with businesses to produce promotional contents (photos, videos, 360° photo/virtual tour, animations and graphics) for advertisement and brand promotion.
